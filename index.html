Privacy Policy for Capy Voice

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Capy Voice ("we," "our," or "us") is an application developed by an individual developer. We respect your privacy and design our app to be privacy-first. We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data, audio recordings, or transcripts. All data processing happens either locally on your device or directly between your device and the third-party AI providers you authorize.

2. Information Usage

2.1 No Proprietary Data Collection

  • We do not have access to your microphone data, API keys, or text transcripts.
  • The App runs entirely client-side on your macOS device.

2.2 Audio Data

  • Microphone Usage: We access your microphone only when you explicitly trigger the recording feature (e.g., via hotkey).
  • Processing:
    • If "Native Speech Engine" is enabled, audio is processed on your device by Apple's Speech Recognition service.
    • If "OpenAI Whisper" is enabled, audio data is sent to OpenAI's API for transcription.

2.3 Text Data & AI Interaction

When you use the "AI Response" or "Improve Grammar" features, the transcribed text and any prompts you provide are sent to Google's Gemini API (Generative AI) for processing.

2.4 API Keys

Your API keys (OpenAI, Google Gemini) are stored locally on your device in the Secure Keychain. We do not have access to your keys, and they are sent directly from your device to the respective third-party providers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide the App's core functionality. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:

  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy (Used for Whisper transcription)
  • Google Gemini: https://policies.google.com/privacy (Used for AI intelligence)
  • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ (Used for Native Dictation)

4. Data Retention

  • Capy Voice App: Since we do not operate a server, it is physically impossible for us to retain your data. Audio recordings and transcripts are processed transiently in memory and are discarded immediately after use.
  • Third Parties: Third-party providers (OpenAI, Google) may retain API data in accordance with their own data retention policies.

5. Your Consent

By using the App and entering your API keys, you consent to the transmission of your audio and text data to these third-party services for the purpose of fulfilling your requests.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
